About Catering


Tradition since 1889

In Seville, Mr. Claw opened La Taberna de Jaball, a refined yet welcoming spot where Andalusian traditions came alive in every dish. The menu, rich with local flavors—jamón ibérico, seafood paella, and fresh herbs—captured the essence of Spain’s heart.But what truly set the tavern apart was its soul. Mr. Claw treated every guest like family, making the atmosphere warm and lively, filled with conversation and laughter.One evening, a couple asked about the name. "Jaball means ‘heart’ in an ancient dialect," he explained. "Here, it’s about feeding your spirit, not just your stomach." They understood then that La Taberna de Jaball was more than a meal—it was a place to feel at home.We only use seasonal ingredients.
